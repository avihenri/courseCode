Campgrounds in Yosemite

The following campgrounds are open all year.

Wawona

93 sites with space for RVs and trailers up to 35 feet. Groceries available from the small store in Wawona.

One mile north of Wawona on the Wawona Road.

Hodgdon Meadow

105 sites with space for RVs up to 35 feet and trailers up to 27 feet. Groceries available from the small store at Crane Flat. Otherwise, stock up in Yosemite Valley.

Adjacent to the Big Oak Flat Entrance Station about 45 minutes northwest of Yosemite Valley off the Big Oak Flat Road (Highway 120).
